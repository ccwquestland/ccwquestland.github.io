Indah Damansara Condominium

Indah damansara Condomium, Damansara Heights, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

9-storey condominium development with reinforced concrete beam and column construction, founded on concrete bored piles, located on a visibly high ground and along Jalan Damansara Endah, Damansara Heights.

Developed by Pembinaan Sebato Sdn Bhd.

Completed in 1992.
