Robson Heights Condominium

Robson Heights Condominium, Taman Seputeh, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

21-storey luxury condominium development staggered along a hilly terrain.

Reinforced concrete column and beam structure on concrete bored piles. Reinforced concrete retaining walls were judiciously configured and constructed to provide a stable structural system.

Developed by the Unico Group.

Completed in 1994.
