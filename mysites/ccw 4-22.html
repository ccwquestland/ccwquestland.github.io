PJ8, Jalan Barat, Petaling Jaya, Malaysia

Block C is a 20-storey office building within the mixed development of residential and office buildings forming PJ8.

Conventional reinforced concrete beam and column frame structure, using large diameter concre bored piles of 1200mm to 1800mm diameters.

Developed by IJM Properties Sdn Bhd.

Completed in 2008.
