Pines Condominium

Pines Condominium, Brickfields, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

27-storey condominium development with a separate annex facilities block.

Founded on concrete bored piles, with conventional reinforced concrete column and beam frame structure.

Developed by Gemilang Cergas Sdn Bhd of the Berjaya Group.

Completed in 1993.
