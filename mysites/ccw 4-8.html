Tribeca

Tribeca Serviced Apartments, Jalan Imbi, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

37-storey building comprising 318 units of luxury serviced apartments.

The building is founded on concrete bored piles in a predominantly limestone site, with reinforced concrete shear walls sitting on a post-tensioned transfer plate at Level 9.

Developed by Bakti Dinamik Sdn Bhd of the Lowyat Group.

Completed in 2017.
