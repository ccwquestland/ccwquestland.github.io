Sungei Buloh Industrial Park

Sungei Buloh Industrial Park, Sungei Buloh, Malaysia

Infrastructure works for 120 acres of land, one of the earliest industrial park development in Sungei Buloh.

Scope of work included earthworks, road and drains, retainings, an elevated water storage reservoir and its own sewerage treatment plant.

Developed by Azabar Holdings Sdn Bhd.

Infrastrucure works were completed in 1993.
