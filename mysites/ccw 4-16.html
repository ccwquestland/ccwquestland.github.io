Ampwalk

Ampwalk, Jalan Ampang, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Twin 10-storey blocks of mixed development with shopping and office space.

Reinforced concrete structure founded on a combination of concrete bored piles and micro-piles in a largely limestone site.

Developed by Permata Alasan (M) Sdn Bhd, a joint set-up by Tan & Tan Developments Berhad and Wearne Brothers Properties (pte) Ltd.

Completed in 1999.
