PPR Lembah subang

PPR Lembah subang, Subang, Petaling Jaya, Malaysia

Eight blocks of 16 to 17-storey low-cost apartments totalling 3004 units. Buildings are reinforced concrete shear wall system founded on precast concrete spun piles.

Developed by FACB/CTRM Consortium under the Ministry of Housing Malaysia.

Completed in 2001.
