Dayabumi Office Complex

Dayabumi Office Complex, Jalan Hishamuddiin, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

40-storey building comprising a steel structural frame as the superstructure, uniquely embedded in reinforced concrete columns and concrete lift core walls in the basement substructure. The reinforced concrete basement substructure formed part of the substructure contract involving concrete bored piling works.

Developed by Urban Development Authoroity (UDA).

Completed in 1984 (while Ir Dr Wong was Technical director of HKHC).
