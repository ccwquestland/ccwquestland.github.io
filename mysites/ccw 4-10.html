The Gardens Midvalley

The Gardens Midvalley, Lingkaran Syed Putra, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

The 36-storey Gardens Hotel, the 40-storey South Office Tower and the 40-storey North Office Twoer part of the mixed development forming the Mid-Valley city.

The Gardens Hotel is a shear wall structure seated on transfer beams on top of the retail floors and basement car parks below.The North and south office towers are provided with column free central office space, spanning between the central cores and the perimeter columns. The buildings are founded on concrete bored piles.

Developed by Mid-Valley Gardens Sdn Bhd of the IGB Group.

Completed in 2008.
