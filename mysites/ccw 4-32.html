Menara Pinang

Menara Pinang, Jalan Kia Peng, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

23-storey condominium development consisting of 108 units of spacious apartment units.

Reinforced concrete beam frame structure with precast hollow core floor system, and founded on concrete bored piles.

Developed by Song Pang Seng Realty Sdn Bhd.

Completed in 1992.
