Impian Emas

Impian Emas, Bangsar, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

8-storey condominium development constructed on a steep hillside on Jalan Kapas, Bangsar, Kuala Lumpur.

This reinforced concrete column and beam structure frame is founded on concrete bored piles.

Developed by Nasbina Sdn Bhd.

Completed in 1994.
