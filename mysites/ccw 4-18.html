Gleneagles Medical Centre

Gleneagles Medical Centre, Jalan Ampang, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

8-storey 300-bed hospital with an adjoining medical office building.

Conventional reinforced concrete and column and beam structure, founded on precast concrete piles in a limestone site.

Originally developed by Tan & Tan Developments Bhd & Gleneagles Hospital (KL) Sdn Bhd.

Completed in 1996.
