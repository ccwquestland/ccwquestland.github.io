Casa Vista

Casa Vista, Jalan Kapas, Bangsar, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Twin blocks of 20-storey condominium units seated on a hill slope, with an overall drop of 40m between Jalan Kapas and Jalan Penaga in Bangsar, Kuala Lumpur.

Reinforced concrete structure on concrete bored pile foundation, constructed with the bored piles providing intersecting shear resistance into the hillside to enhance the stability required.

Developed by Mutiara Tegas Sdn Bhd, a joint venture between Seacorp and the Pilecon Group.

Completed in 1993.
