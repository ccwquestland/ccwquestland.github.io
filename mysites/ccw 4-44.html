UTM Student Hostels

UTM Student Hostels in Taman Setapak Jaya, Setapak, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Four blocks of 8-storey buildings fronting a man-made artificial lake.

The buildings are founded on precast concrete square piles and are built on reclaimed land.

A turnkey project by Panji Bersatu Sdn Bhd and developed by Universiti Technologi Malaysia.

Completed in 2003.
